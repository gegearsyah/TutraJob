## âœï¸ Writing Tips

### Headlines
- Be clear and specific
- Use numbers/lists (5 Tips, 3 Ways)
- Start with action verb
- Maximum 60 characters

### Body Copy
- Use short paragraphs (3-4 lines)
- Clear formatting with headings
- Include call-to-action
- Proofread for typos

### Testimonials
- Keep authentic
- Specific details (job title, achievement)
- Emotional tone (impact + gratitude)
- Include permission from author

### Descriptions
- Lead with benefit
- Answer "why" not just "what"
- Use simple language
- 1-2 sentences ideal
